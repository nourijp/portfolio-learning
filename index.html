About

I build learning materials at the intersection of writing, visual communication, product thinking, and educational technology. My work includes training guides, learner-facing resources, app-based practice systems, quizzes, instructional media, and AI-assisted prototypes. I am especially interested in learning experiences that are clear, structured, culturally thoughtful, and easy to use.

Based in Denton, TX.
